  • Key Technical Decisions

    Three decisions that closed the gap

    Action-Gate Residual Warm-start from Max-Pressure, learn when to deviate. Gate override fraction: 12% → 30%.
    Person-Weighted Reward Per-person delay normalization. Removed throughput term that dominated gradients and encouraged proxy gaming.
    4-Scenario Curriculum Round-robin: AM peak, PM peak, demand surge, midday multimodal. 8 parallel SUMO workers, LR scaled by 1/√8.
